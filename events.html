Upcoming Events

Book Signing with Local Authors

Meet and connect with some of your favorite local authors. This event will feature readings, Q&A sessions, and a chance to get your books personally signed. Enjoy a warm and inspiring atmosphere as you discover new and exciting stories. Refreshments will be served, and you'll have the opportunity to engage in one-on-one conversations with the authors, learning about their creative processes and inspirations. It's an event you won't want to miss!

Date: Saturday, April 20th

Children's Story Time

Gather your little ones for a fun-filled story time session. Our experienced storytellers will captivate your children with classic tales and modern favorites, sparking their imaginations and fostering a love for reading. This is a perfect weekend activity for the entire family. In addition to storytelling, there will be interactive activities, including sing-alongs, crafts, and games designed to keep your children entertained and engaged throughout the session. Snacks will also be provided to keep everyone happy!

Date: Sunday, April 28th

Poetry Reading Night

Immerse yourself in the art of spoken word during our Poetry Reading Night. Enjoy powerful and thought-provoking poems from local poets, and participate in an open mic session to share your own work. This event is all about celebrating creativity and community. Whether you are a seasoned poet or a first-time performer, everyone is welcome to take the stage. The event will also feature live acoustic music to set the mood, making it a perfect evening to connect with fellow poetry enthusiasts.

Date: Friday, May 10th

Summer Book Fair

Don't miss our annual Summer Book Fair! Enjoy exclusive discounts on books, meet your favorite authors, and participate in fun activities for all ages. This is a great opportunity to stock up on summer reads and support the local literary community. There will be book readings, workshops for aspiring writers, and a children's corner with fun activities. Special guests will include popular authors who will be signing books and answering your questions. Food trucks and live music will make this an event to remember!

Date: Saturday, June 15th
